Steps for Brewing

Gather your ingredients, and be sure your equipment is sanitized. You will need: Water, a Brew Kettle, Malt Extract, Hops, and Yeast

Fill brew kettle with required amount of water accoring to recipe, bring to a boil

Once water is boiling, turn off heat and add liquid and dry extract, return to boil, and follow hop addition time table provided in recipe.

After boil, cool using a water bath or immersion chiller as fast as possible, down to around 70 degrees F. Pitch yeast, and put in carboy for around 2 weeks, then bottle or keg.
